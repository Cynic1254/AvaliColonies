### Function
Farms crops for your colony.
Also crafts seeds, carved pumpkin, hay bales and coarse dirt

The Farmer's Hut requires Fields to function, The field block tells the farmer what seeds to plant and how big to make the farm
### Levels

2. 20 recipes, 2 Fields
3. 40 recipes, 3 Fields
4. 80 recipes, 4 Fields
5. 160 recipes, 5 Fields
