### Function
Citizens will go to the Residence they are assigned to at night to sleep. The Residence is also a way to get more citizens for your colony
### Levels
1. 1 Citizen
2. 2 Citizens
3. 3 Citizens
4. 4 Citizens
5. 5 Citizens
### Research
To have more than 25 Citizens in the colony you will need to research `Outpost` `Hamlet` `Village` and `City`
### Limits
The housing limits are enforced on a code level
