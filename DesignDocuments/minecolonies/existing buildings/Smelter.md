### Function
The Smeltery is where the Smelter will smelt ores into ingots.
### Levels
1. Smelter applies no fortune
2. Smelter applies fortune 1
3. Smelter applies fortune 2
4. Smelter applies fortune 3
5. Smelter applies fortune 4
### Research
Requires the `Hot!` research to build
### Skills
- primary: Athletics
- Secondary: Strength
### Limits
The amount of furnaces a smelter can use is calculated by `PrimarySkill / 10 + 1`
