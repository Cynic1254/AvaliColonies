### Function
Produce enchanted Books from Ancient Tome's
Can also craft some magic scrolls upon request
- The Ultrasafe Colony Teleport Scroll
- The Ultrasafe Colony Group Teleport Scroll
- The Spatial Guard Reinforcements Scroll
- The Worker-Where-Are-You Scroll
### Levels
1. Level 1 enchantment with 50% odds
2. Level 2 enchantment with 25% odds
3. Level 3 enchantment with 15% odds
4. Level 4 enchantment with 5% odds
5. Level 5 enchantment with 1% odds

### Research
The Spatial Guard Reinforcements Scroll and The Worker-Where-Are-You Scroll require the `More Scolls` research to be crafted

### Skills
- primary: Mana
- Secondary: Knowledge

### Limits
Enchantments and enchantment levels are fully limited through loot tables
