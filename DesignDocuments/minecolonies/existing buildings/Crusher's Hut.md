### Function
The Crusher's Hut is where the Crusher will take items and crush them into other blocks:
Bone into Bonemeal
Bone Block into Bonemeal
Cobblestone into Gravel (and flint)
Clay into Clay Ball
Gravel into Sand
Sand into Clay
