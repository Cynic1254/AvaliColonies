### Function
The Graveyard is where the Undertaker will bury your deceased citizens.
Once the Undertaker gets to the Graveyard , they will attempt to revive the deceased citizen.
If the citizen cannot be revived, the Undertaker will bury them in the Graveyard .

### Research
This building requires the `Rememberance` research
Revive chance can be increased with dedicated research
Using totems to increase the revive chance is unlocked with research
### Skills
- primary: Strength
- Secondary: Mana
### Limits
The resurrection chance can be increased by the following:
- Research (+1 and 2%)
- The undertaker's secondary skill (+0.125% per skill point)
- The level of the graveyard (+0.5% per Level)
- The use of totems (+5% for 1 totem, +7.5% for 2 totems), totems have a 1% chance on breaking.
By default the revival chance is capped at 2.5% (ignoring any extra chance added through totems) but can be increased by building a [[Mystical Site]] which increases the chance by .5% per level
