### Function
The Plantation is where the Planter will grow a variety of plants:
- Sugar Cane
- Cactus
- Bamboo
- Cocoa Beans
- Vine
- Kelp
- Seagrass
- Sea Pickles
- Glow Berries
- Weeping Vines
- Twisted Vines
- Crimson Plants (Crimson Roots and Crimson Fungus)
- Warped Plants (Warped Roots and Warped Fungus)
The plantation uses Plantation Fields which have to be build differently based on the preferred plant.
The Planter can also craft paper, books, sugar, and anything made with bamboo.
### Levels
1. 1 max fields, 1 max plant types, 10 recipes
2. 1 max fields, 1 max plant types, 20 recipes
3. 2 max fields, 2 max plant types, 40 recipes
4. 2 max fields, 2 max plant types, 80 recipes
5. 3 max fields, 3 max plant types, 160 recipes
### Research
Requires the `Let it grow` research to be build
With the `Crop Rotation` research the max fields for any given level is increased by 1
### Skills
- primary: Agility
- Secondary: Dexterity
