### Function

Train archer skills, while training archers will not be classified as "Guards" and will not defend the village.

While training the archers will use the Archery as their home requiring enough beds to house all students.
### Levels
1. 1 student
2. 2 students
3. 3 students
4. 4 students
5. 5 students

### Research
This building is unlocked through the `Improved Bows` research

### Skills
- primary: Agility
- Secondary: Adaptability

### Limits
Only a single target and shooting place is needed
