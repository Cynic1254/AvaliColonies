### Function
Barracks server as a container for [[Barracks Tower]] the barracks also allow players to hire 'spies' who will warn them about incoming raids

### Levels
1. Max Barrack Tower level of 1
2. Max Barrack Tower level of 2
3. Max Barrack Tower level of 3
4. Max Barrack Tower level of 4
5. Max Barrack Tower level of 5

### Research
This building is unlocked through the `Tactic Training` research

### Skills
- Archer:
	- Primary: Agility
	- Secondary: Adaptability
- Druid:
	- Primary: Mana
	- Secondary: Focus
- Knight:
	- Primary: Adaptability
	- Secondary: Stamina

### Limits
The maximum number of Barrack Towers is fully controlled by the schematic
