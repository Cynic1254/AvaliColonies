### Function
Dig a mine underneath the building and gather resources from it.
If the mine is used to hire a quarrier to work in the [[Quarry]], no mine will be created
### Levels
The miner will be able to dig 1 level down per building levels, the levels are based on where the mine is placed and are referenced as follows:
- y48
- y16
- y-16
- bedrock
for example if the mine is placed below y level 48, the first level it mines at will be y level 16
