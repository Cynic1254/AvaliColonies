### Function
The blacksmith hut crafts tools, armour swords and shields.

### Levels
1. 10 maximum recipes
2. 20 maximum recipes
3. 40 maximum recipes
4. 80 maximum recipes
5. 160 maximum recipes - Unlocks the ability to craft netherite items

### Research
This building is unlocked through the `Hitting iron!` research

### Skills
- primary: Strength
- Secondary: Focus
