### Function
The Stonemason's Hut is where the Stonemason will craft 3x3 recipes made entirely out of cobblestone, stone, andesite, diorite, granite, quartz, purpur, nether bricks, prismarine, sandstones, blackstone, basalt, and/or ores (no ingots or redstone items). They can also craft end stone from cobblestone and an ender pearl
### Levels
1. 10 recipes
2. 20 recipes
3. 40 recipes
4. 80 recipes
5. 160 recipes
### Research
The end stone recipe can only be crafted when the `Know the end` research has been completed
### Skills
- primary: Creativity
- Secondary: Dexterity
