### Function
The Glassblower's Hut is where the Glassblower will smelt sand into glass and make glass panes from glass blocks.
### Levels
1. 10 recipes
2. 20 recipes
3. 40 recipes
4. 80 recipes
5. 160 recipes
### Research
This building requires the `Those Lungs` research
### Skills
- primary: Creativity
- Secondary: Focus

### Limits
The maximum usable furnace count by the worker is calculated as `PrimarySkill / 10 + 1`
