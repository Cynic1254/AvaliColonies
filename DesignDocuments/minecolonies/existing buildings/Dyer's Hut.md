### Function
Craft dyes and dye other items, including red nether bricks and dark prismarine.
### Levels
1. 10 max recipes
2. 20 max recipes
3. 40 max recipes
4. 80 max recipes
5. 160 max recipes

### Research
This building requires `Rainbow Heaven` to build

### Skills
- primary: Creativity
- Secondary: Dexterity

### Limits
The maximum number of furnaces a dyer can use is `PrimarySkill / 10 + 1`
