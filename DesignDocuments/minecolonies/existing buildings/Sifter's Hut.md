### Function
The Sifter's Hut is where the Sifter will sift through dirt, gravel, sand, or soul sand to find loot.
Dirt:
- Beetroot seeds
- Carrots
- Melon seeds
- Potatoes
- Pumpkin Seeds
- Oak Saplings
- Spruce Saplings
- Birch Saplings
- Jungle Saplings
- Acacia Saplings
- Dark oak Saplings
- Wheat seeds
Gravel:
- Coal
- Diamonds
- Lapis Lazuli
- Emeralds
- Flint
- Gold ingots
- Iron ingots
- Iron nuggets
- Redstone
Sand:
- Cacti
- Cocoa beans
- Gold nuggets
- Sugarcane
Soul Sand:
- Blaze powder
- Glowstone dust
- Magma cream
- Nether wart
- Quarts
- Human skulls
You can choose between four Meshes. The higher the level of the mesh, the higher the likelihood that the Sifter will find loot.
### Levels
1. 64 max blocks sifted per day
2. 256 max blocks sifted per day
3. 576 max blocks sifted per day
4. 1024 max blocks sifted per day
5. Unlimited max blocks sifted per day
### Research
You must research `Sieving` to build this building
### Skills
- primary: Focus
- Secondary: Strength
