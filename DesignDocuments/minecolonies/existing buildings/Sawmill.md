### Function
The Sawmill is where the Carpenter will craft any items made of at least 75% wood
### Levels
1. 10 maximum recipes
2. 20 maximum recipes
3. 40 maximum recipes
4. 80 maximum recipes
5. 160 maximum recipes
### Research
You must have the `Woodworking` research to build this building
### Skills
- primary: Knowledge
- Secondary: Dexterity
### Limits
