### Function
Harvests honeycombs *or* honey bottles

### Levels
1. 1 Beehive
2. 2 Beehives
3. 4 Beehives
4. 8 Beehives
5. 16 Beehives

### Skills
- primary: Dexterity
- Secondary: Adaptability

### Limits
Beehive limits are fully enforced through the beekeeper tool.

A Beekeeper will only try to breed bees up to `BuildingLevel * 3` which is equivalent to 1 hive worth of bees per level
