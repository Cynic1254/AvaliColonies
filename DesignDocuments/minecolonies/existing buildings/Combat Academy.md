### Function

Train knight skills, while training knights will not be classified as "Guards" and will not defend the village.

While training the knights will use the Combat Academy as their home requiring enough beds to house all students.
### Levels
1. 1 student
2. 2 students
3. 3 students
4. 4 students
5. 5 students

### Research
This building is unlocked through the `Improved Swords` research

### Skills
- primary: Adaptability
- Secondary: Stamina

### Limits
Only a single practice dummy is required for training
